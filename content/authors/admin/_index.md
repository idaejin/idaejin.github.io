I am assistant professor in Data Science and researcher at the [School of Science & Technology](https://www.ie.edu/school-science-technology/) at [IE University](https://www.ie.edu/) and Chief Scientist of the [Health & Tech Lab](https://www.impactxcelerator.org/healthtech) at [ImpactXcelerator](https://www.impactxcelerator.org/).

Before joining IE University, from 2014 to 2022, I was a researcher at [BCAM - Basque Center for Applied Mathematics](http://www.bcamath.org/en/) in Bilbao, where I led the research line in Applied Statistics and coordinated the area of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence of the Knowledge Transfer Unit at the Center.

From 2011 to 2014, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at [CSIRO](https://www.csiro.au/en/) (the Commonwealth Industrial and Research Organization), Division of Mathematics and Statistics, today Data61, in Melbourne, Australia.

My current research is Biostatistics, statistical modelling with applications in Health, biomedical applications, environmental sciences and sports sciences.
